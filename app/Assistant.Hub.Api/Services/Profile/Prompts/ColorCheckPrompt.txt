## COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS:

The label on the immediate package of a hazardous product, and any outer wrapping or container that might cover up the label on the package, must meet the following criteria:

(1) Colors that color-blind may confuse should not be close to each other on the label.
(2) The label should not rely on color to convey information.
(3) The label should use a color scheme that is color-blind friendly.
